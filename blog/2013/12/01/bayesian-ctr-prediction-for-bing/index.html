Bayesian CTR Prediction for Bing

Dec 1st, 2013

Microsoft published a paper in ICML 2009 named ‘Web-Scale Bayesian Click-Through Rate Prediction for Sponsored Search Advertising in Microsoft’s Bing Search Engine’, which is claimed won the competition of most accurate and scalable CTR predictor across MS.

I like it because it’s totally based on Bayesian, and Bayesian is beautiful. 1). we can get one distribution of prediction, instead of one single ‘probability’. This feature is valuable for ‘exploration and exploitation’ issue. 2). Also because it’s based on Bayesian, this model naturally support online learning; 3). It’s fast and has good enough prediction accuracy; Btw: one shortage of this model is it’s not sparse, which may be a big issue when applied on big dataset with huge amount of features.
